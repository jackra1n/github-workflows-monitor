Missing organizations

There are 2 most common problems if it comes to missing organizations.

  • 1. Application has no access to the organization
  • 2. Your organization isnt public on your profile

If your organization only has public repositories then the easiest way to solve the problem is to just make the organization public on your profile. If your organization isn't public then the app will have to get access to your organization.

If you're the organization owner then you can click "Grant" button next to your organization when you're loggin to this application. If you're member of a organization you will see "Requst" button instead and a organization owner will be informed that you want to use this app.
